Lemon Caper Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter

Step 1

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

Step 2

In a large skillet, melt butter over low heat. Stir in lemon juice and capers. Cook for 5 minutes on low.
