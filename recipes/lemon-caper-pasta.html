Lemon Caper Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over low heat. Stir in lemon juice and capers. Cook for 5 minutes on low.
  3. Toss pasta with butter sauce. Serve with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Enjoy!
